The Platform

Built for
modern demand.

The Green Market bridges the gap between regenerative farms and your table, delivering nutrient-dense food with a carbon-neutral footprint.

01

Farm to Table (Actually)

We directly track produce from harvest to shipment. No warehouses, no multiple handlers. Just smart logistics connecting you with local growers within a 50-mile radius.

02

Predicted Harvesting

Our ML model analyzes hyper-local weather data to predict the peak nutrient window for every vegetable, ensuring we harvest only when quality is at 100%.

03

Waste-Free Supply Chain

We operate on demand. By matching harvests to active orders, we reduce supply chain food waste by 98% compared to traditional grocery models.
